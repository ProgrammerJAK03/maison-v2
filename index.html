The PowerSeal Process

We use state-of-the-art technology to ensure your cylinder lasts longer and performs better than original. Here is how we do it.

01. Inspection

We chemically clean and ultrasonically inspect your cylinder to ensure it's a candidate for replating.

02. Bore & Prep

The old plating is removed, and the cylinder is precision bored to the correct Nikasil specifications.

03. Nikasil Plating

We apply a nickel-silicon-carbide composite coating in our controlled electro-plating tank.

04. Honing & Finish

The cylinder is diamond honed to exact piston clearance for optimal ring seating.
